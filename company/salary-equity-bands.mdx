Use bands to set expectations by level and role, and to drive internal equity.

## Configure bands

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Company → Salary & Equity Bands">
Add ranges by role, level, location, and currency.
</Step>

<Step title="Create a band">
Click “Add Salary & Equity Band,” enter role, level, and ranges, then Save.
</Step>

<Step title="Upload CSV (optional)">
Use “Upload CSV” to add multiple bands at once. Download the sample to see the format.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Use bands during offer creation

<Info>
We surface band ranges during offer creation to guide decisions. You can still tailor offers to candidates.
</Info>

<Info>
Bands help ensure fairness across teams and reduce back‑and‑forth during approvals.
</Info>
