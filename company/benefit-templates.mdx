Templates save time and ensure consistent language across offers.

## Create templates

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Company → Benefits">
Manage your standard company benefits.
</Step>

<Step title="Add a template">
Click “Add Benefit” and fill in Type, Name, and Description. Optionally add a URL or upload a PDF.
</Step>

<Step title="Edit or delete">
Use the row actions to update or remove a template.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Use templates in offers

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Import into an offer" icon="inbox">
In the Benefits tab, click “Import from Templates.” We skip duplicates.
</Card>

<Card title="Customize after import" icon="pen-to-square">
Edit wording, add plan links, or attach PDFs specific to the role.
</Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
Keep template names concise so they read well on the offer page.
</Tip>
