Upload your logo and set brand colors to personalize offers.

## Add your branding

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Company → Branding">
You’ll see sections for Logo, Primary Color, and Header Image.
</Step>

<Step title="Upload your logo">
Click “Upload Logo” (or Change Logo). A live preview appears. Remove with the X.

<Tip>
Use a high‑resolution transparent PNG or SVG for best results.
</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Pick a primary color">
Enter a hex code or use the color picker. This color styles your offer header and accents.
</Step>

<Step title="Add a header image (optional)">
Upload a banner image for the offer header. Remove or replace any time.
</Step>

<Step title="Save changes">
Click “Save Changes”. We’ll apply your branding to previews and candidate views.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Tips

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Recommended file formats">
Prefer SVG or PNG with transparency. Max size 10MB.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Color accessibility">
Choose colors with sufficient contrast for readability.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
