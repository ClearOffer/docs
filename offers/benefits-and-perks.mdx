Use the Benefits tab to highlight your total rewards. Benefits are optional but recommended.

## Add or import benefits

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Add New Benefit" icon="circle-plus">
Create custom items with descriptions and optional links or PDFs.
</Card>

<Card title="Import from Templates" icon="inbox">
Quickly add company‑standard benefits in one click. We avoid duplicates.
</Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
You can drag and drop benefits to reorder how they appear.
</Info>

## Best practices

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Clear naming">
Keep names short and clear (e.g., “Medical PPO 90”).
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Explain the value">
Add a sentence on coverage, eligibility, or highlights.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Attach docs where helpful">
Link to plan docs or attach a summary PDF candidates can download.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Save and continue

Click “Save Offer” to finish or return to earlier tabs to adjust details.
