## What you’ll see

<CardGroup cols={2}>

Role, Department, Reports To, Start Date, Location, and Expiration.
</Card>

<Card title="Compensation" icon="money-bill">
Base salary and payment frequency; bonuses and commission when provided.
</Card>

<Card title="Equity" icon="chart-column">
Type, shares, prices, vesting and cliff when provided.
</Card>

<Card title="Benefits" icon="gift">
List of benefits you add or import, in the order you choose.
</Card>
</CardGroup>
