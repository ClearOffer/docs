Enter company valuation and context to help explain equity. This data can enable ownership display and provide helpful context in candidate views.

## Add valuation

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Company → Valuation & Comparables">
Enable “Show Company Valuation in Offers” if you want to display valuation and ownership.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter valuation and shares">
Add current valuation (USD) and Outstanding Shares to calculate ownership.
</Step>

<Step title="Enable comparable companies (optional)">
Maintain a list of comparable companies with stage, valuation, date, and comments. Add private notes for your team and public comments for candidates.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Where it’s used

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Ownership display" icon="percent">
Show estimated ownership for equity grants based on valuation and outstanding shares.
</Card>
<Card title="Candidate context" icon="comments">
Use Comparables and public comments to help candidates understand company stage and growth.
</Card>
</CardGroup>
