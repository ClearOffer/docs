When you’re invited, you’ll receive an email with a secure link to join your company on ClearOffer.

## Join your company

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the invite link">
Click the link in your email. You’ll land on the Accept Invitation page.
</Step>

<Step title="Sign in or create an account">
If you don’t have an account yet, you’ll be prompted to create one first.
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm">
Review the company name and click Accept to join the workspace.

<Check>
You’ll be redirected to the Dashboard with access based on your role.
</Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Invitation issues

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="The link is invalid or expired">
Ask your admin to resend the invitation. Links expire for security.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I joined the wrong company">
Contact your admin or support to remove the incorrect association.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
