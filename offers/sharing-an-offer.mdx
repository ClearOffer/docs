When you’re ready to send, create a secure link and share it with the candidate.

## Generate a link

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Share dialog">
From the offer page, click <strong>Share</strong> to open “Shareable Offer Link”.
</Step>

<Step title="Copy the link">
Click the copy button to copy the secure link.
</Step>

<Step title="Send it">
Share via your preferred channel (email, ATS, or chat).
</Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Links are unique to each offer and can be regenerated if needed.
</Info>

- Include your contact details in the Welcome Message for easy replies
