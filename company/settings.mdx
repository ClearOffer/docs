Find core settings under Company → Settings.

## Common settings

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Company info" icon="id-card">
Name and a short description shown in offers.
</Card>

<Card title="NDA before viewing" icon="shield-check">
Toggle “Require NDA Before Viewing Offers.” If enabled, customize the NDA template presented to candidates.
</Card>

<Card title="Default offer settings" icon="money-bill">
Default equity type (Options/RSUs), strike price, preferred price, and default payment frequency.
</Card>

<Card title="Valuation display" icon="chart-column">
Control whether valuation and ownership appear in offers (configured under Valuation & Comparables).
</Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
These settings help pre‑fill new offers and improve consistency across your team.
</Tip>
