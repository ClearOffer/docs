## Log in

- Go to `https://clearoffer.io/login`
- Enter your email and password
- If your email isn’t verified yet, we’ll guide you to complete verification

## Reset your password

<Steps>
<Step title="Start reset">
Click “Forgot password?” on the login screen.
</Step>

<Step title="Check your email">
We’ll send a reset link. Click it to open the reset form.
</Step>

<Step title="Create a new password">
Choose a strong password and submit.

<Check>
You can now log in with your new password.
</Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Didn’t receive the email">
Check spam and filters. If needed, request another link after a few minutes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Reset link expired">
Start a new reset from the login page.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
