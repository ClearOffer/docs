Create your workspace in minutes and invite your team.

## Create your account

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to signup">
Visit `https://clearoffer.io/signup` and enter your work email and password.
</Step>

<Step title="Check your inbox">
We send a verification link to your email. Click the link to verify your account.

<Info>
Didn’t get it? Wait 1–2 minutes, then check your spam folder.
</Info>
</Step>

<Step title="Finish setup">
After verifying, you’ll be redirected to sign in.

<Check>
You can now access the Dashboard and invite teammates.
</Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="My verification link expired">
Return to the app and request a new verification email from the banner or the verification page.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Wrong email or a typo">
Sign out and repeat signup with the correct email. If the account already exists, use password reset.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Corporate filters blocked the email">
Ask IT to allowlist `clearoffer.io` and resend the verification.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
